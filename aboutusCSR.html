OUR VISION

Operating with excellence, safety

At Mustang Helicopter, safety is our foundation, precision is our discipline, and excellence is the standard we rise to every single day.

The vision of Mustang Helicopter is to lead Nepal’s aviation sector with safe, efficient, and innovative helicopter services that strengthen connectivity across remote regions. Our values center on uncompromising safety, customer-focused service, integrity, innovation, sustainability, and teamwork.

We aspire to elevate Nepal’s aviation frontier through meticulous craftsmanship, disciplined operational ethos, and a culture that honours continuous learning and technological advancement. Our commitment extends beyond transportation, we endeavor to be a lifeline to remote communities, a bridge to spiritual destinations, and a dependable force in humanitarian and emergency missions.

MESSAGE FROM

THE CHAIRMAN

For us, safety is not merely a priority—it is the bedrock on which every mission stands

As the Chairman of Mustang Helicopter, I bring to the helm an illustrious 16-year tenure in military service, honed within the rigorous confines of the Special Forces. This unparalleled experience has instilled in me an indomitable discipline, an unyielding focus on precision, and a resolute commitment to leadership—all of which underpin my approach to achieving excellence in every facet of our operations.

In the unforgiving realms of the mountains, I have faced challenges that demanded split-second decision-making, where the safety of the team rested entirely on the strength of my resolve. This same steadfast dedication to safety forms the cornerstone of my role at Mustang Helicopter. For us, safety is not merely a priority—it is the bedrock upon which every aspect of our mission is built.

Equipped with the most advanced fleet of helicopters, Mustang Helicopter operates under the meticulous guidance of the highest safety standards prescribed by the Civil Aviation Authority of Nepal (CAAN). Our team of dedicated professionals spares no effort in ensuring that every protocol is adhered to with absolute precision. We take no shortcuts; every operation is executed with meticulous care, leaving no room for compromise.
