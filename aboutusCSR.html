about us / Our Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Programs

Mustang Life Flight CSR Program

Our wings are not just built to fly — they are built to uplift communities and empower future generations

At Mustang Helicopter Pvt. Ltd, we believe that aviation must serve humanity beyond business. Our CSR initiatives are designed to save lives, uplift communities, empower future generations, and promote inclusive growth within Nepal’s aviation sector. Our programs are strategically aligned with national priorities and the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (UN SDGs).

Postpartum Emergency Medical Evacuation Program

This is Mustang Helicopter’s flagship humanitarian CSR initiative, dedicated to saving the lives of mothers and newborns in Nepal’s most remote and mountainous regions

Through this program, we are committed to provide:

  • Subsidized emergency helicopter evacuations for postpartum mothers
  • Priority access to tertiary hospitals during life-threatening emergencies

This program directly contributes to reducing maternal and neonatal mortality, making Mustang Helicopter a proud partner in Nepal’s national health mission.

Flying to save motherhood

Flying dreams into reality

Aviation Scholarship & Youth Training Program

To strengthen Nepal’s future aviation workforce, Mustang Helicopter actively invests in young talent through

  • Scholarships for aspiring pilots, aircraft engineers (AME), and aviation managers
  • Internship and on-the-job training programs
  • Mentorship by experienced aviation professionals

This initiative builds national capacity while creating equal opportunities for deserving youths across Nepal.

Women in Aviation Empowerment Program

A rare and powerful CSR initiative in Nepal, this program is dedicated to empowering women to build careers in aviation. We provide professional training for women as:

  • Flight Dispatchers
  • Aircraft Engineers
  • Operations Officers
  • Career mentoring, internships, and skill-based employment pathways
  • Leadership exposure within aviation operations

This program directly supports gender equality, workforce diversity, and women’s economic empowerment, aligning strongly with UN Sustainable Development Goal 5 (Gender Equality).

Women leading Nepal's skies

OUR COMMITMENT

Mustang Helicopter Pvt. Ltd. remains committed to building a responsible aviation ecosystem that places human life, national service, and inclusive development above all else. Through our CSR programs, we fly not only passengers—but also hope, care, opportunity, and resilience across Nepal's skies.
